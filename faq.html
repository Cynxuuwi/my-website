2. What made you choose Information Systems as your course?

3. Do you plan to pursue a career in gaming or esports?

While I enjoy gaming and esports, my primary focus is on pursuing a career in technology. However, I do plan to continue competing in esports as a hobby.

4. How do you balance your studies and gaming?

Balancing studies and gaming requires discipline. I make sure to prioritize my academic work and use gaming as a way to relax during my free time.
